Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

StatesNG is an independent educational reference application. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or associated with the Federal Government of Nigeria, any State Government, Local Government, Ministry, Department, Agency, or any other government institution.

Overview

StatesNG is an offline educational application that provides reference information about Nigeria's 36 States and the Federal Capital Territory (FCT). Protecting your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

StatesNG does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The application does not:

  • Collect your name, email address or phone number.
  • Collect your location.
  • Access your contacts, camera, microphone or files.
  • Track your activities.
  • Use analytics services.
  • Use advertising SDKs.
  • Create user accounts.
  • Require registration or login.

Internet Connection

StatesNG is designed to work completely offline. The educational content is bundled with the application and does not require an internet connection for normal use.

Data Storage

StatesNG does not store any personal information on your device or on remote servers.

Educational information contained in the application is stored locally as part of the application package.

Data Sharing

Because no personal information is collected, no user information is shared, sold, rented, or disclosed to any third party.

Children's Privacy

StatesNG is suitable for users of all ages, including children. Since no personal information is collected, the application does not knowingly collect information from children or adults.

Security

Since StatesNG does not collect or transmit personal information, there is no personal user data stored on external servers.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if our privacy practices change. Any updates will be reflected by revising the "Last Updated" date on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer through the contact information provided on the Google Play Store listing.
